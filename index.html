Welcome to Weight Tracker

Our goal is to help you stay healthy. Use our simple tool below to calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) and track your fitness journey.

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m². It is used to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

Healthy Tips

To maintain a healthy weight, try to drink 8 glasses of water a day, sleep at least 7 hours, and walk for 30 minutes daily. Consistency is key to long-term health.

Why Track?

Tracking your weight allows you to see your progress over time. Whether you want to lose weight or build muscle, knowing your numbers helps you stay motivated and focused.
